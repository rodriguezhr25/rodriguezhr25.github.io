Target audience

The audience for this website is defined for young adults or parents who are interested in find information about the weather conditions in the caribbean destinies for vacations, family trips and tourism. Their age are between 18-60 years old. They search information from their cellphones in Google about when is the best time and location to take a trip to the Caribbean.
